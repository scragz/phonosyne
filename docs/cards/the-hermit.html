The Hermit isolates in a frozen echo

He walked into the snowfall, a lantern dangling from a horned staff of dead light. The wind made no sound, it listened. Beneath the frost-bitten canopy, his breath summoned tone from silence. The world retreated, each flake a data point in divine solitude. He did not seek truth, he cultivated its absence. His song was a whisper to the roots, and the roots remembered.
